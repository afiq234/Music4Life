About Us

Who music4life?


Music4life is a Malaysia audio streaming and media services provider, founded in 2006 by Afiq and Farzani. Music4life is incorporated in Kuala Lumpur as Music4life Technology S.A, and is headquartered in Putrajaya, Malaysia, with offices in 17 different countries around the world. It is one of the world's largest music streaming service providers, with over 356 million monthly active users, including 158 million paying subscribers, as of March 2021.

Why music4life?


With music4life, it’s easy to find the right music for every moment – on your phone, your computer, your tablet and more. There are millions of tracks and episodes on music4life. So whether you’re behind the wheel, working out, partying or relaxing, the right music is always at your fingertips. Choose what you want to listen to, or let music4life surprise you. You can also browse through the collections of friends, artists, and celebrities, or create a radio station and just sit back.
